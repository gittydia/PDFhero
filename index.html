An app called PDFhero aids pupils in their efficient study sessions. Among the features that PDFhero provides are concise and well-organized explanations of the PDF information that you provide, notes that are created for you using AI technology, notes that are explained as if you were five years old, and a pretest. If you want to learn the topic simply upload the file and tap the ask ai button, it will direct you to our ai "Hero". Hero will help you understand the concept better. Finally, if you want to test your knowledge tap the Test button and our Ai will give you test according to the pdf you upload.
